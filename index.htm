User Experience

Children of Digital Age is a business in the education field: our purpose is to form today's children to be tomorrow's programmers. Since IT is an ever-growing field there is a lot of potential in knowing programming from earlier ages. However, learning it can be a sterile attempt frail with failures. This is why our business plans to teach children how to code using interactive approaches, making them learn by doing and having fun. The site we're going to build is just the interface through which both children and parents can find out about the business and can enroll in future classes.
